About

M.Tech graduate in Machine Learning from DA-IICT, currently working as a Software Developer at IBM (Gift City, Gandhinagar). My work spans the full ML lifecycle — from fine-tuning LLMs with LoRA and RLHF, to building computer-vision pipelines with Grad-CAM explainability, to shipping production-ready full-stack applications with FastAPI, React, and Streamlit.

I built Link & Think, a local RAG-powered study assistant that streams cited answers from any uploaded document, video, or URL; and CerebroScan AI, a dual-model brain MRI classifier that gives radiologists an interpretable second opinion via attention heatmaps. On the LLM side I fine-tuned Mistral-7B for financial QA using Q-LoRA, landing within 4% of GPT-3.5-turbo under constrained resources.

Beyond code, I served as a Teaching Assistant at DA-IICT (Algorithms & Probability), write ML-focused articles on Medium, and took 1st place at the Smart Gujarat Hackathon (Medium Scale Category). I'm always looking for problems where careful ML engineering makes a real difference.
