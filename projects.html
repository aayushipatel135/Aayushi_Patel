CerebroScan AI — Brain Tumor MRI Classification

TensorFlow / Keras EfficientNetB0 Custom CNN Grad-CAM Streamlit Transfer Learning Computer Vision
  • Deep-learning pipeline classifying brain MRI scans into 4 categories — Glioma, Meningioma, Pituitary tumor, and No Tumor
  • Dual-model approach: custom 3-block CNN trained from scratch vs. EfficientNetB0 fine-tuned with a 2-stage transfer learning strategy (frozen feature extraction → selective backbone unfreezing at 1e-5 lr)
  • Grad-CAM explainability layer highlights the exact MRI regions driving each prediction, providing radiologists with an interpretable second opinion
  • Optimised for Macro Sensitivity over accuracy — minimising false negatives (missed tumors) as the primary success criterion
  • End-to-end Streamlit dashboard: upload any MRI image, switch between models, view per-class confidence bar chart, and toggle the Grad-CAM heatmap
  • Full preprocessing pipeline with data augmentation; model weights saved to disk and loaded on demand by the dashboard
